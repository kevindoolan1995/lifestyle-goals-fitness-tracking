⚖️ The 10 Laws
1
Never miss Monday. Push day anchors the week.
2
Track your lifts every session. If you don't track, you don't progress.
3
Hit 180g protein every single day. Muscle is built in the kitchen.
4
Sleep 8 hours. Non-negotiable. Growth hormone releases during deep sleep.
5
Prep your meals Sunday. No exceptions. 2 hours Sunday = a perfect week of eating.
6
Add weight or reps every week. Progressive overload is the entire game.
7
Eat on rest days too. Your body rebuilds muscle on rest days.
8
No alcohol during a growth phase. Alcohol tanks testosterone and destroys recovery.
9
Don't change the plan for 12 weeks. Program hopping kills progress.
10
Your workout is a meeting you never cancel. You show up. Always.
